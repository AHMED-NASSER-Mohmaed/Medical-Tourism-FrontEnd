Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about medical tourism with MediTour

Our medical advisors evaluate your specific needs and match you with the most appropriate facilities based on:

  • Specialty expertise and success rates for your procedure
  • Hospital accreditation and international certifications
  • Patient reviews and outcomes data
  • Language support and international patient services

We provide detailed profiles of each option to help you make an informed decision.

Our comprehensive packages typically include:

  • Medical procedure costs (surgeon fees, facility charges, implants if applicable)
  • Pre-treatment consultations and diagnostic tests
  • Hospital stay and nursing care
  • Accommodation (hotel or recovery center)
  • Airport transfers and local transportation
  • 24/7 patient support and concierge services
  • Follow-up consultations and telemedicine access

Packages can be customized based on your specific needs and preferences.

Savings vary by procedure and country of origin, but typical savings range from:

  • Cardiac procedures: 60-75% savings
  • Orthopedic surgeries: 50-70% savings
  • Dental treatments: 60-80% savings
  • Cosmetic surgeries: 40-65% savings

Even after including travel and accommodation expenses, patients typically save thousands of dollars while receiving care at internationally accredited facilities.

Patient safety is our top priority. We ensure:

  • All partner hospitals are JCI accredited or meet equivalent international standards
  • Board-certified surgeons with extensive international experience
  • Strict infection control protocols and modern facilities
  • Comprehensive medical malpractice insurance coverage
  • Patient advocates who accompany you to all medical appointments
  • Emergency support available 24/7 during your stay

We also verify all credentials and track patient outcomes to maintain our high standards.
